Enumeration type for ADC Scale values Internal ADC channels automatically use the most appropriate scale.

Enumerator

MXC_ADC_SCALE_1 

ADC Scale by 1x (no scaling)

MXC_ADC_SCALE_2

MXC_ADC_SCALE_3 

ADC Scale by 1/3.

MXC_ADC_SCALE_4 

ADC Scale by 1/4.

MXC_ADC_SCALE_6 

ADC Scale by 1/6 (this uses 1/3 and an additional 1/2 scaling)

MXC_ADC_SCALE_8 

ADC Scale by 1/8 (this uses 1/4 and an additional 1/2 scaling)
